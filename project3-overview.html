Electric Vehicle Population - Project Overview

Problem

The overall goal of this project was to analyze the level of adaption through electric vehicles (AKA EV's) in primarily Washington State. Along with this, analyzing and identifying trends and patterns will help to reveal how much electric vehicle use had grown, and share where adaption is most common.

Data

The dataset includes variables such as the EV's make, year, and model, as well as location and eligibility by registration. Data Wrangling and preprocessing was conducted in order to produce the best results before applying a LightGBM Model.

Approach

The main platform used was Google Colab, incorporating Exploratory Data Analysis, machine learning, and visuals in order to point out key trends for the adaption of EV's in Washington State.

Key Findings

  • EV adaption seems to have the biggest percentage of adaption in the Urban areas.
  • Certain makes and models of EV's are more common given their geographical area..
  • Based off the patterns, the growth and distribution of EV's will continue to slowly increase over time.

Main Tools/Applications Used

  • Google Colab
  • Python
  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Plotly
