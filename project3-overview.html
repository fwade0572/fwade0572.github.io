Electric Vehicle Population - Project Overview

Problem

The overall goal of this project was to analyze the adoption of electric vehicles in Washington State and identify any trends or patterns that could help in understanding how electric vehicle usage has grown, along with where adoption is most common.

Data

The dataset includes variables such as electric vehicle make, model, model year, vehicle type, location, and eligibility information. Before making any conclusions, data wrangling and preprocessing was conducted to establish the best possible results.

Approach

The main platform used for the project was Google Colab, along with performing exploratory data analysis, machine learning, and visualizations that could identify key trends related to electric vehicle adoption in Washington State.

Key Findings

  • Electric vehicle adoption varies across different areas in Washington State.
  • Certain makes and models appear more commonly within the electric vehicle population.
  • The patterns revealed can help better understand the growth and distribution of electric vehicles.

Main Tools/Applications Used

  • Google Colab
  • Python
  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Plotly
