Out of the three roles: Data Wrangler, Scientist, and/or Visualizer, I acted as the Data Wrangler. This included taking both the red and white wine datasets and combining them before ultimately cleaning, transforming, and assessing it all in one main source. This way, it makes a more efficient and valuable effort to be sent over to the data scientist for training and test splitting the data; and then eventually the visualizer for final submission.
