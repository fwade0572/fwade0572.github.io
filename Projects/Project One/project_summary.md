The first group project I partook a role in consisted of Crime in LA.  Our main goal for this was to determine the age and time of day impact the victim demogrpahics most, and what public safety procedures
could be put into place to decrease the overall crime rates.  

Out of the three roles, data wrangler, scientist, and/or visualizer, I acted as the data visualizer. This included taking the cleaned data from the wrangler and constructing a logistic regression model in order to form
visualizations with a high accuracy rate.  With these visuals, we were able to answer our primary question, along with secondary ones that assisted our solutions.
